Cumorah Jewelry - Site Plan

Site Name

Cumorah Jewelry

This name represents the unique experience of acquiring jewelry with Colombian emeralds, making each purchase a memorable treasure.

Available domain: cumorahjewelry.com

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide a secure purchasing option for jewelry with Colombian emeralds, offering certificates of origin and authenticity. The menu will include options to buy rings, bracelets, earrings, charms, and emeralds.

Scenarios

  • How can I verify the authenticity of an emerald I want to buy?
  • What types of jewelry are available at Cumorah Jewelry?

Color Schema

  • Main Color: #4B4B4B (Dark Gray, used for headings)
  • Secondary Color: #FFFFFF (White, used for background and text)
  • Accent Color: #C2B280 (Light Earth Brown, used for accents)

Typography

Main Font: Roboto, sans-serif (Used throughout the site)
